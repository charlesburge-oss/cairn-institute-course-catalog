# BHA-101 — Behavioral Health Awareness

## Modules
1. Behavioral Health Foundations
2. Mental Health and Substance-Use Awareness
3. Stigma and Respectful Communication
4. Recognizing Possible Distress
5. Crisis and Emergency Referral
6. Trauma-Informed Support
7. Boundaries and Scope of Practice
8. Community Resources
9. Self-Care and Resilience
10. Supporting Recovery

## Capstone
Develop a nonclinical support and referral response for a fictional situation.

## Important Limitation
This course does not prepare learners to diagnose, treat, counsel, or provide licensed behavioral-health services.
