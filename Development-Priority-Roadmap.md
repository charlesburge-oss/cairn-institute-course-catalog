# Course Development Priority Roadmap

This roadmap sequences development so the institute orientation courses and IT Foundations pathway are built and piloted first, then their approved Moodle and curriculum structure is reused for the remaining academies.

## Phase 1 — Immediate Development
CAIRN-ORI-100, CAIRN-DIG-100, CAIRN-ETH-100, TECH-101, TECH-110, TECH-120, TECH-130, TECH-140, CYB-101, WRK-101, CAR-101, LEA-101.

## Phase 2 — Human Services and Organizational Programs
VAC-101, DVA-101, CAD-101, SHO-101, SAA-201, HRA-101, FIN-101, GRA-101, FRA-101, OPS-101.

## Phase 3 — Research, AI, Data, and Advanced Technology
AIA-101, DAT-101, RES-101, TEC-101, TECH-150, TECH-160, TECH-170, TECH-180, CYB-201, AIN-201.

## Phase 4 — Community and Specialized Academies
Develop the remaining public health, emergency management, youth leadership, faith partnership, sustainability, public service, reentry, and community-development courses after qualified reviewers and program owners are identified. This includes PHA-101, EMA-101, BHA-101, PSA-101, COM-101, FCP-101, FCP-110, SUS-101, YLA-101, YLA-110, YLA-120, MIL-101, GOV-101, JRA-101, ESB-101, FCR-101, BUS-101, EDU-101, EDU-201, WRK-201, ORG-201, PRO-101, RMA-201, and HTR-201.

## Notes

This gives CAIRN a proposed starting catalog of foundational courses, academy courses, shared courses, and certificate pathways. The strongest implementation sequence is to complete the institute orientation and IT Foundations pathway first, then reuse that approved Moodle and curriculum structure for the other academies. All courses remain PROPOSED and require academic and subject-matter review before publication or pilot delivery.
