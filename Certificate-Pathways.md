# Recommended Certificate Pathways

Certificate pathways combine institute-wide orientation courses with school-specific foundations courses and a capstone. All pathways are proposed and require academic and subject-matter review before publication.

## Certificate in IT Foundations
CAIRN-ORI-100, CAIRN-DIG-100, TECH-101, TECH-110, TECH-120, TECH-130, TECH-140, CYB-101, TECH-150, plus a career portfolio and capstone.

## Certificate in Human Services Foundations
CAIRN-ORI-100, CAIRN-ETH-100, VAC-101, CAD-101, ELA-101, FSA-101, BHA-101, plus a human-services capstone.

## Certificate in Shelter and Advocacy Services
VAC-101, DVA-101, SHO-101, CAD-101, SAA-201, FCP-110, plus a shelter-services capstone.

## Certificate in Organizational Leadership
LEA-101, HRA-101, FIN-101, OPS-101, CAIRN-ETH-100, ORG-201, plus an organizational leadership capstone.

## Certificate in Grant and Fundraising Development
GRA-101, FRA-101, FIN-101, RES-101, DAT-101, plus a grant-development capstone.

## Certificate in AI and Data Foundations
CAIRN-DIG-100, AIA-101, DAT-101, RES-101, AIN-201, TEC-101, plus an AI and data capstone.

## Certificate in Cybersecurity Foundations
TECH-101, TECH-120, TECH-130, CYB-101, CYB-201, TECH-150, plus a cybersecurity capstone.

## Certificate in Workforce and Career Development
WRK-101, CAR-101, EDU-101, WRK-201, LEA-101, DAT-101, plus a workforce-program capstone.
